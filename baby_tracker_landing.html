What the app adds

Everything the spreadsheet does, plus this.

Your spreadsheet is great for logging and reviewing. The app is for right now — at 3am, one handed, half asleep.

⏱️

Live feed & nap timers

Tap to start, tap to stop. Duration saves automatically. No typing while feeding.

🔔

Feed reminders

Set how often you want a reminder. The app notifies you even when the screen is off.

👫

Shared with your partner

Both phones sync to the same Google Sheet. You can both see what happened and who did it.

Setup

Ready in 5 minutes

1

Open the app

Tap the button below. The app opens in your browser — no App Store needed.

2

Add to your home screen

iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". Android: tap ⋮ → "Add to Home Screen". Now it works like a real app.

3

Connect your Google Sheet

The app walks you through a 3-step setup. Create a free Google Sheet, paste the sync script, and you're connected. Full guide included in your download — or see tidystack.com.au/baby-tracker-setup.

4

Share with your partner

Open the app on their phone, tap "Set up sync", paste the same Script URL. Use a different name so you can see who logged what.
